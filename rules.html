General Rules

1

Be Respectful

Treat all players, staff members, and community members with respect. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated.

2

No Spam or Advertising

Do not spam chat with repetitive messages, excessive caps, or character spam. Advertising other servers or services is strictly prohibited.

3

English in Global Chat

Please use English in global chat to ensure everyone can communicate effectively. You may use other languages in private messages.

4

No Inappropriate Content

Do not share inappropriate, offensive, or NSFW content in chat, skins, usernames, or any other form of communication.

Gameplay Rules

5

No Cheating or Hacking

The use of hacked clients, cheat modifications, or any form of unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. This includes but is not limited to: Kill Aura, Reach, Fly, Speed, X-Ray, and AutoClicker.

6

No Exploiting or Bug Abuse

Exploiting bugs or glitches to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed. If you discover a bug, please report it to staff immediately.

7

No Teaming in FFA

Teaming in Free-For-All game modes is prohibited. Play fairly and individually as intended.

8

Play to Win

Always play competitively and fairly. Throwing matches, stat boosting, or win trading will result in penalties.

Account Rules

9

No Alt Accounts for Ban Evasion

Using alternate accounts to evade bans or mutes is strictly prohibited and will result in permanent bans on all accounts.

10

Account Security

You are responsible for the security of your account. Do not share your account credentials with anyone.

Punishment Guidelines

Warning

First-time minor offenses may result in a warning from staff members.

Temporary Mute

Chat violations will result in temporary mutes ranging from 1 hour to 7 days.

Temporary Ban

Moderate violations will result in temporary bans from 1 day to 30 days.

Permanent Ban

Severe violations including cheating, severe harassment, or repeated offenses will result in permanent bans.

Think you were wrongly punished? You can appeal your punishment by joining our Discord server and opening a ticket.
